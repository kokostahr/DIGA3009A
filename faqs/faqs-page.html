Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? Maybe you'll find the answers here.

How do I submit a tip?

Click the “Submit a Tip” button on the homepage or forum page. You’ll be guided from there.

Can I post anonymously?

Yes. You can choose to post with a username or anonymously. Your privacy matters to us.

What kind of content is allowed?

Tips, questions, crew hacks, layover stories, and anything that helps the aviation community thrive.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Click the flag icon on any post or comment. Our team will review it promptly. UNFORTUNATELY, it doesn't yet Exist but know that we're working around the clock to bring new features to the site.
