About Cabin Clue

Cabin Clue is a space for crew to connect, share, and survive aviation life... together (or solo, if you're not into the whole 'we are one' discourse).

Why It Exists?

We (the devs) believe that crew life is chaotic, and worth documenting. Cabin Clue was built to hold your questions, tips, and layover secrets with care.

Who It's For?

Cabin crew, ground staff, aviation students, and anyone navigating the skies. If you’ve ever packed snacks in your uniform pocket or dream about exploring the world, then this is for you -`♡´-.

How It Works?

Ask questions. Share tip; Save items, and connect. Cabin Clue is your space, so make it more comfy than the airport lounges.
